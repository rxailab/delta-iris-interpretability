Fig. 4 Single-step causal ablation. Change in log-likelihood of the observed codes at the target step when the concept’s SAE direction is projected out of the block-1 residual stream, at the target step itself (left) or three steps earlier (right); n = 80 moments per bar (26–29 for two concepts), error bars are bootstrap 95% CIs. Blue: matched direction at unlock moments. Magenta: matched direction at ordinary moments. Grey: random direction at unlock moments. Markers show the mean change; whiskers are bootstrap 95% CIs. Effects are direction- and moment-selective at gap 0 and vanish entirely at gap 3.
